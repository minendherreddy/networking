SOHO -- small office home office network.
There are three types of commnication:
 1) Electrical communication.
 2) optical communication.
 3) wireless communication.
Bandwidth -- the number of bits transfered per second.
Throughput -- the maximum amount of data being transfered it is as same as bandwidth but it denpends upon some factors:
        1) the amount of data transfered
        2) the type of the data.
        3) the latency -- the total amount of time taken to transfer the data.
